The shore's #1 request

Flood elevation certificates that protect your premium.

On a barrier island, a few inches of elevation can mean thousands of dollars a year in flood insurance. Our FEMA Elevation Certificates document exactly where your lowest floor sits relative to the Base Flood Elevation — the number your insurer, lender and town all rely on.

  • Current FEMA form, completed by a licensed surveyor
  • Shoots lowest floor, grade & mechanicals to NAVD 88 datum
  • Supports LOMA / LOMR-F requests to remove a flood zone
  • The documentation lenders and the NFIP require to rate your policy
