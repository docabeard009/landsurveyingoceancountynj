Point Pleasant Beach sits at the northern edge of Ocean County by the Manasquan Inlet, blending a busy boardwalk district with established residential streets. Many Point Pleasant Beach properties — especially near the ocean and Manasquan Inlet — are in high-risk FEMA flood zones such as AE or VE. An elevation certificate documents your home's elevation for flood insurance.
