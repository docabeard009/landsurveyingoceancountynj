Local questions

Surveying in Brick Township, NJ

Are Brick's lagoon homes in flood zones?+

Waterfront and lagoon-front properties in Brick are commonly mapped in FEMA flood zone AE. An elevation certificate documents your home's elevation for insurance and permitting purposes.

Do you handle waterfront boundary and bulkhead questions?+

Yes. Where lots meet bulkheads, lagoons or the river, boundary and riparian lines can be complex. A sealed survey clarifies exactly where your property ends.

Can you survey inland Brick neighborhoods too?+

Of course. We handle boundary, title and topographic surveys throughout Brick — waterfront and inland.
