Surveying in Toms River, NJ

Do all Toms River properties need flood elevation certificates?+

No — inland Toms River neighborhoods may sit in lower-risk zones, while bayfront lagoon homes and the Ortley Beach section are typically in high-risk zones. We can confirm your zone and whether a certificate is warranted.

Do you survey the lagoon and bayfront communities?+

Yes. Waterfront lots along Barnegat Bay often involve flood, bulkhead and riparian considerations, and we handle the boundary, elevation and topographic work those properties require.

Can you handle a closing survey on a deadline in Toms River?+

Absolutely. Meeting closing deadlines is what attorneys count on us for. Tell us the date when you request your quote.
