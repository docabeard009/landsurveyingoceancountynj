Local questions

Surveying in Long Beach Island, NJ

Is all of Long Beach Island in a flood zone?+

Nearly all of LBI is mapped within FEMA flood zones, commonly AE or VE depending on location. An elevation certificate is typically needed to properly rate flood insurance on island property.

Do you serve all LBI towns?+

Yes — Ship Bottom, Surf City, Harvey Cedars, Barnegat Light, Beach Haven and the surrounding communities. We provide flood, boundary, title and construction surveys island-wide.

Can you meet a closing deadline on LBI?+

Yes. The LBI market moves quickly, and timely delivery for closings is a core part of how we work. Share your closing date when you request a quote.
