Local questions

Surveying in Spring Lake, NJ

Is my Spring Lake home in a flood zone?+

Most of Spring Lake is mapped within a FEMA flood zone — commonly AE, with VE along the oceanfront. A flood elevation certificate documents exactly how your lowest floor sits relative to the Base Flood Elevation, which drives your insurance rating.

Can a survey lower my flood insurance in Spring Lake?+

Often, yes. If your lowest floor is at or above the Base Flood Elevation, an elevation certificate — and sometimes a LOMA — can reduce your premium or remove a high-risk flood-zone requirement. We prepare the data both rely on.

How fast can you get a survey done in Spring Lake?+

Fast — our crew works out of Lavallette, so the shore is our home turf. For closings and policy deadlines we routinely turn elevation certificates and boundary surveys on a tight schedule.
