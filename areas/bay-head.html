Why does boundary precision matter so much in Bay Head?+

With high property values and closely-held oceanfront and bayfront lots, even small boundary or easement questions can carry significant financial and legal weight. A sealed survey provides the certainty owners and attorneys need.

Are Bay Head oceanfront homes in Zone VE?+

Oceanfront properties in Bay Head are frequently mapped in coastal high-hazard Zone VE, with bayfront lots often in AE. Elevation certificates document the figures insurers and lenders require.
