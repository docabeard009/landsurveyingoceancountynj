Ortley Beach — a barrier-island section of Toms River Township — was among the areas most heavily damaged in 2012, and the wave of elevated rebuilds that followed has never fully stopped. Local questions

Surveying in Ortley Beach, NJ

Do I need a new elevation certificate after elevating my Ortley Beach home?+

Yes. Once a home is raised, a new elevation certificate documents the finished-floor elevation relative to the Base Flood Elevation — the figure that determines your flood premium and may qualify you for significant savings.

Can you lay out piles for an elevated rebuild?+

Yes. Elevated, pile-supported construction is standard in Ortley Beach. We stake pile locations and elevations tied to your approved plans and the flood requirements for the lot.

Is Ortley Beach in Toms River?+

Yes — Ortley Beach is the barrier-island section of Toms River Township. We survey both the island section and the Toms River mainland.
