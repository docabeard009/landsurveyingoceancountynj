Surveying in Seaside Heights, NJ

Why are flood elevation certificates so common in Seaside Heights?+

Much of Seaside Heights is in high-risk flood zones, with oceanfront blocks often in Zone VE. Lenders and the NFIP require elevation data to rate flood policies, so the certificate is one of the most-requested surveys in the borough.

Do you handle investment and rental property surveys here?+

Yes. Seaside Heights has heavy rental and investment activity, and we provide the boundary, title and elevation surveys those purchases and refinancings require.

How is boundary work different on Seaside's tight lots?+

Closely-spaced structures make encroachments and exact line locations more likely to matter. A sealed boundary survey removes the guesswork before you build, buy or resolve a dispute.
